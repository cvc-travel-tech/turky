Who are we and Who are we not ?

Tareek AlOfuk Co is an Iraqi Travel and Tourism company.
Tareek Al Ofuk provides tourism services with high standards sinds 2012.
We are not a company which does other business besides tourism. We are just doing tourism nothing else.
Now We are also serving at the promised City of Istanbul in Turkey with 12096 TURSAB (Turkish Tour Operators Association) license.

Why Tareek Al Ofuk

• Always delivering the highest quality of tours. All the time having best partner companies, with well-known hotel chains, luxury private tours and bosphorus cruises, finest transportation enterprises and much more.
• We are delighted to making your trip sure is impeccable by taking care of every detail of your dream vacation. Our professionals knows how you to get best value of your time and joy.
• Various of tours in Istanbul City is offered to meet your expectations. Whether you have only read about your destination or you are in deep discovery, we ensure you will find the right tailored accomodations and tours for your needs.

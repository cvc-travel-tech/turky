Start/End (Cairo/Cairo)

 

Day 1, Arrival at Cairo, Optional Sound and Light Show (Saturday)
• After the arrival to Cairo International Airport, we offer the "meet and greet" service. Afterwards, our customer will be transferred to the hotel for check in. • In the evening, the Optional Sound and Light Show in front of the three pyramids of Giza plateau is suggested for a memorable night.
Day 2, Cairo, Khan El-Khalili, Egyptian Museum (Sunday)
• Start the day with a visit to the famous bazaar or market Khan El-Khalili in the historic center of Cairo. • After lunch, we proceed to the Egyptian Museum in El-Tahrir Square, the home of Egypt's most valuable antiquities. • Meals served: Breakfast and Lunch.
Day 3, Travel to Luxor, Optional Tour to Dendarah Temple (Monday)
• Early morning, you will arrive at Cairo International Airport to catch the flight to Luxor, and then transfer to your luxuries Nile Cruise where you will spend the next seven nights. • You have the day free at your leisure OR you may choose our Optional Half-day visit to the Temple of Dendara. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and dinner.
Day 4, Optional Hot Air Balloon Ride, Luxor West Bank Tour, Esna (Tuesday)
• As the sun rises over Luxor, we offer an Optional Tour to enjoy hot air balloon flight. • Breakfast will be served on the Nile Cruise, after which you will visit Luxor West Bank necropolis including the Valley of the Kings, the Mortuary Temple of Queen Hatshepsut and Colossi of Memnon. • Back to the Nile Cruise for having lunch and enjoying the silence of the Nile at night while sailing to Esna. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
Day 5, Edfu, Kom Ombo (Wednesday)
• Early morning, we will sail to Edfu and visit Temple of Edfu, then head to Kom Ombo for overnight. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
Day 6, Kom Ombo, Aswan, Optional Sound and Light Show (Thursday)
• Sail to Aswan; stop at The Aswan High Dam and afterwards Philae Temple. • Take a felucca in the Nile and sail around The Botanical Garden, and then come back to the Nile Cruise for overnight. • At night you may choose the Optional Sound and Light show. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
Day 7, Aswan, Optional Abu Simbel (Friday)
• You have the day free at your leisure OR you may choose our Optional Abu Simbel Tour. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
Day 8, Aswan, Kom Ombo, Edfu, Esna (Saturday)
• Early morning, arrive at the Temple of Kom Ombo. • Sail to Esna for overnight. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
Day 9, Luxor East Bank (Sunday)
• Sail to Luxor, and then you will visit Luxor East Bank monuments beginning with the outstanding Karnak Temple followed by Temple of Luxor. • Meals served: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
Day 10, Luxor, Travel to Cairo (Monday)
• After having breakfast, check out from the cruise and you may leave your luggage at the front desk to enjoy free morning at Luxor city. • Transfer to Luxor Airport for your flight to Cairo. • Arrive to Cairo and check in your hotel. • Meals served: Breakfast.
Day 11, Great Pyramids, Memphis, Sakkara (Tuesday)
Our day starts with a visit to the one and only standing monument among the seven wonders of the ancient world, the Great Pyramid of Khufu, followed by The Pyramids of Khafra & Menkaura, the well-preserved valley temple of Khafra and the Great Sphinx. • The next stop will be a nice restaurant for having lunch. Our tour will continue to Memphis, south west of Giza pyramids, the 1st capital of Ancient Egypt. • The last stop of our tour will be The Pyramid Complex of Sakkara. • Meals served: Breakfast and Lunch.
Day 12, Old Cairo, the Citadel (Wednesday)
Start the day by a visit to Old Cairo, also known as the "Center of Religions". • Stop at The Citadel of Saladin. • Meals served: Breakfast and Lunch.
Day 13, Cairo, Optional Alexandria Tour (Thursday)
• You have the day free at your leisure OR you may choose our Optional Tour to Alexandria by private car. Visit Alexandria National Museum, the catacomb of Kom El-Shoqafa, the Ancient Roman Amphitheater and finally arrive at Bibliotheca Alexandrina. • Return to Cairo for overnight. • Meals served: Breakfast.
Day 14, Departure (Friday)
• Transfer to Cairo International Airport for Departure after having breakfast at the hotel. • Meals served: Breakfast.
